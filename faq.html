Frequently Asked Questions

What is contract farming?

Contract farming is an agreement between farmers and buyers to produce a specific crop under agreed-upon terms, ensuring both parties' needs are met.

How do I join the platform?

You can join by clicking the "Join Now" button on the homepage and filling out the registration form.

What types of crops can I contract?

Our platform supports a variety of crops. You can specify your preferred crops during the contract creation process.

Is there a fee for using the service?

No, joining and using our platform is completely free for farmers and buyers.

How can I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team via the contact form on our website or through the contact information provided in the footer.
