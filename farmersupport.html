Support Services for Farmers

Financial Aid

Farmers can apply for various government schemes that provide financial assistance, including subsidies for seeds, fertilizers, and equipment.

  • Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi: Direct income support for farmers.
  • Soil Health Card Scheme: Financial support for soil testing and management.

Agricultural Resources

Access to information about best farming practices, crop rotation, pest control, and organic farming methods.

  • ICAR Resources: Educational materials and research publications.
  • Krishi Vigyan Kendras: Local agricultural training centers for farmers.

Contact Support

If you need assistance or have questions, you can reach out to the following resources:

  • Agricultural Helpline: 1800-XXXX-XXX
  • Email Support: support@farmersupport.in
  • Local Extension Officer: Visit your nearest agricultural office.
